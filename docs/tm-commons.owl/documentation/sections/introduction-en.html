Introduction back to ToC

A set of 5 ontology modules (also known as ontologies in an ontology network) has been defined, as discussed at the beginning of this section. The choice of modules and their content is only partially based on the original non-ontological resources, since we did not want to bias the way in which all the ontology components are organised and represented. Therefore, we decided to use a number of modules that is in between those used by NeTEx and Transmodel, and which we think that make sense from a functional point of view, taking into consideration our representation needs:

  • Organisations (prefix tmorg, https://w3id.org/transmodel/organisations#) to represent all the information related to the public transport organisations.
  • Fares (prefix tmfare, https://w3id.org/transmodel/fares#) to represent all the information related to fares.
  • Facilities (prefix tmfac, https://w3id.org/transmodel/facilities#) to represent all the information related to facilities.
  • Journeys (prefix tmjourney, https://w3id.org/transmodel/journeys#) to represent all the information related to the trips and the different types of journeys for passengers and vehicles.
  • Commons (prefix tmcommons, https://w3id.org/transmodel/commons#) to represent all those concepts and properties that are general enough to be used across all modules.
